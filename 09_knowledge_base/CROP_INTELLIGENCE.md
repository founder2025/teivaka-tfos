**Coverage:** All 49 productions in the Teivaka system
**Market data:** Current Fiji market (FJD), 2025-2026

---

## Notes on This Document

- All prices are in Fijian Dollar (FJD). Approximate USD conversion: FJD 1.00 ≈ USD 0.44.
- "Active in Teivaka System" refers to active production cycles as of system launch date.
- Yield ranges are per acre, based on Fiji smallholder conditions (not industrial).
- Fiji has two seasons: **Wet Season (Nov–Apr)** and **Dry Season (May–Oct)**.
- Kadavu (F002) is island-based with higher humidity and more reliable rainfall year-round.
- Local Fijian names are included where they are in common agricultural use.
- For active crops, PU codes are listed (e.g., F001-PU002 = Farm 001, Production Unit 002).

**Active crops are marked with** ★
