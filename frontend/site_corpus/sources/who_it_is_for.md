# Who is Teivaka for?

TFOS serves farmers operating at different levels of operational complexity through progressively surfaced workflows built on the same infrastructure layer.

A farmer only interacts with the level of complexity they currently need. As the farm grows, the platform reveals more.

## Solo

Solo is designed for smallholders and first-time digital users operating in low-connectivity environments.

The interface focuses on guided execution:

- one task at a time,
- voice-assisted instructions,
- minimal reading,
- and low cognitive load.

The goal is not software mastery. The goal is helping the farmer consistently complete the next operational task while structured operational history is built quietly in the background.

A farmer with limited digital literacy can begin using Solo with minimal onboarding and very little technical familiarity.

## Growth

Growth supports farmers managing increasing operational complexity:

- multiple crops,
- multiple farm blocks,
- labour activity,
- harvest tracking,
- cashflow visibility,
- and production planning.

This layer introduces broader operational visibility while remaining practical for daily field operations.

The interface is structured around:

- Home,
- Classroom,
- Farm,
- TIS,
- and Me.

Growth is designed for the majority of Pacific working farmers who need operational clarity without enterprise-level complexity.

## Commercial

Commercial supports larger agricultural operations with more advanced operational requirements:

- employees,
- recurring buyers,
- scheduled deliveries,
- compliance workflows,
- forecasting,
- analytics,
- multi-site coordination,
- and operational reporting.

The underlying infrastructure remains the same; additional operational depth becomes available as the farm grows in complexity.

Commercial operators familiar with imported farm management tools should be able to look at TFOS and recognise a system designed around Pacific operational realities rather than adapted from assumptions built somewhere else.

## Progressive Expansion

Farmers are not required to migrate platforms as they grow.

TFOS progressively unlocks additional workflows and operational capabilities based on how the farm operates, preserving continuity of operational history while reducing adoption friction.

A Solo farmer who plants their first managed crop does not switch to Growth — Growth reveals itself. A Growth farmer who hires their first full-time worker does not migrate to Commercial — the additional workflows surface.

## Beyond the Farmer

The same operational record can support multiple stakeholders:

- buyers verifying deliveries,
- extension officers reviewing compliance,
- lenders assessing operational consistency,
- and future agricultural support systems requiring trusted operational data.

TFOS creates a shared operational history layer for Pacific agriculture.
