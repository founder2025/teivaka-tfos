# About the Founder

Teivaka was founded by Uraia Koroi Kama, a Fiji-based farm operator managing active production farms in both Korovou, Tailevu Province, and Kadavu.

The farms — Save-A-Lot and Viyasiyasi — operate as real production environments rather than demonstration sites. Operational problems encountered on those farms directly informed the systems and workflows later developed inside TFOS.

Teivaka was built in response to realities many imported agricultural systems were never designed for:

- low-connectivity farming regions,
- ferry-dependent logistics,
- low digital literacy,
- irregular smallholder cashflow patterns,
- fragmented operational records,
- and Pacific-specific agricultural workflows.

The platform was designed around those constraints from the beginning rather than adapted to them later.

## The Company

Teivaka PTE LTD is a Fiji-registered company building operational infrastructure for Pacific Island agriculture through TFOS — the Teivaka Farm Operating System.

The company's focus is creating trusted operational records that improve visibility, coordination, compliance, traceability, and financial readiness across Pacific agricultural systems.

Everything inside TFOS is built around one principle:

Turning fragmented farm activity into structured, verifiable operational history.
