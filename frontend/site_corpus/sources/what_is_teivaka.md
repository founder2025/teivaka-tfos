# What is Teivaka?

Teivaka is operational infrastructure built for Pacific Island agriculture.

At the centre of the company is TFOS — the Teivaka Farm Operating System — a platform designed for smallholder and commercial farmers working in low-connectivity, operationally fragmented environments across the Pacific.

A farmer opens the app, sees the next task, completes it, and closes the app.

Behind that simple workflow, TFOS builds a structured operational history of the farm: planting, spraying, harvesting, labour activity, deliveries, input purchases, payments, and production cycles. Activity that was previously remembered, estimated, or written on paper becomes structured operational data that can be reviewed, shared, and verified.

That structured record can support:

- farm management,
- reporting,
- compliance,
- procurement,
- traceability,
- and financial readiness.

The platform is designed around real Pacific farming conditions:

- intermittent connectivity,
- low-end Android devices,
- ferry-dependent logistics,
- remote island operations,
- low digital literacy,
- and mixed-scale farming systems.

TFOS is not a marketplace, a cooperative, or a generic farm management application. It is the operational infrastructure layer underneath agricultural execution and agricultural commerce.

Teivaka PTE LTD is a Fiji-registered company based in Korovou, Tailevu Province, founded by Uraia Koroi Kama — a working farm operator managing active production farms in both Korovou and Kadavu.

Every workflow inside TFOS originated from operational realities encountered on real farms before becoming software.

From a working farm. For working farms.
